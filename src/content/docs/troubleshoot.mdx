# Troubleshooting
Sometimes, you cannot connect, or you have errors. Don't worry, this page will help you solve most of the common issues.

## HELP! | I am not whitelisted!

### Have you verified on the Discord server?
If you are not whitelisted, you have to verify on the Discord server.

### Have you given us your correct Minecraft username?
Check if you have given us the correct name in your verification. All cases counts, even caps, and even spaces.

### ave you given us the good version? 
You may have given us the wrong version. check if you have told us your Java or Bedrock username, and if you are on the correct platform.

## HELP! | I have an error when I try to connect!

### Outdated client
Make sure you are on the latest version of Minecraft.

### Timed out
Mainly an internet issue. Try restarting your router, or connecting to a different network.

### Internal Exception: io.netty.handler.timeout.ReadTimeoutException
This is a common error that can be caused by various issues, such as network problems, server issues, or client-side problems.

### Other errors? 
You shall open a ticket on the discord server, and provide us with the error message, and a screenshot of the error if possible. We will try to help you as soon as possible.

## HELP! | I have other issues!
You shall open a ticket on the discord server, and provide us with a detailed description of your issue, and any relevant screenshots or error messages.
